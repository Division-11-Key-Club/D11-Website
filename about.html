Who we are?

The District Board is responsible for carrying out the business of the District. The board is composed of the District Governor, the Executive Board (Treasurer, Secretary, Bulletin Editor, Webmaster, and Executive Assistants) and the Lieutenant Governors of all divisions. The District Governor acts as chairperson of all District Board meetings.

Each year, the Governor, Treasurer, and Secretary are elected at the annual Leadership Training Conference. The Lieutenant Governors are chosen before the Conference through divisional elections.

The Board annually meets for the LTC, Spring, ICON, Fall, K-Family, and MidWinter Board meetings.

  • Planning for LTC (theme, schedule, etc.)
  • Deciding upon the District Projects

  • Represent the District at the International Level
  • Assist any and all members of Key Club
