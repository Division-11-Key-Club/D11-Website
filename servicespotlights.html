What are Service Spotlights?

Service Spotlights are articles written by Key Clubbers that highlight an event, service project, or significant initiative. They are often accompanied by photographs or videos taken at the events. Write about a service project you were involved in, and you can have a chance to be featured in one of our Service Spotlights!
