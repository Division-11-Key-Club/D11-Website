Divisional Training Conference (DTC)

The annual Divisional Training Conference will be held this year by Division 8/8A/11 at Bayside High School (32-24 Coporal Kennedy St, Bayside, New York 11361) on June 14th (9:00 AM - 3:30 PM). The event is one of the largest of the service year! Not only will you be able to learn about Key Club, but you will also have the opportunity to meet Key Clubbers from other divisions and develop your leadership skills! All newly-elected Club Officers are HIGHLY recommended to attend for their respective leadership training workshops! The admission price is $5.

Register here for DTC!

Apply here to host a workshop - Deadline: MONDAY, JUNE 1!

Possible ideas include:

  • Making Meetings Move/Icebreakers
  • Club Officer Workshops: Secretary, Treasurer
  • Guide to Professionalism
  • Fundraising
  • Service Projects (specific or general)
  • Networking and Social Media
  • ...or anything else you'd like!

Workshops

The workshops held at the DTC are presented by experienced Key Clubbers and leaders. From Club Officer workshops to hands-on activities, your day is filled to the brim with opportunities to learn a few tips or ideas for future service projects! If you're interested in hosting a workshop, feel free to apply at the link above! Email all workshops by June 1 to LTGs Kelly Song, Diana Piktel, and Emily Li.

Networking

Are you new to Key Club? Do you want to meet Key Clubbers from other Divisions? With almost a hundred attendees, the DTC is the best place to get out and make some new friends! From Icebreakers to Panels (where you can even talk to a District Board Member!) to fun workshops, you'll definitely return home with memories and a lot more Key Club friends!

Other Perks

From FREE LUNCH to photo opportunities with your new friends and tons of service ideas for the upcoming year, DTC is one Conference you will not want to miss! In addition, this year, for the first time, the Division is offering admission at an incredibly reduced $5!

About Key Club

Key Club is an international student-led organization which provides its members with opportunities to provide service, build character and develop leadership.
